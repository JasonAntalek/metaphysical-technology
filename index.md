Metaphysical Technology is an emerging category of designed technologies, environments, methods, and frameworks that deliberately engage questions, experiences, or relationships traditionally treated as metaphysical and bring them into operational, observable, testable, or governed form.

This site is the public record for defining, testing, challenging, and developing that category.

<div class="callout">
<strong>The limit:</strong> Category membership does not establish that a metaphysical claim is true. It does not confer scientific legitimacy, prove efficacy, guarantee safety, or convert belief into evidence.
</div>
